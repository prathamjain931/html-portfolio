The Best Movies According to Pratham

My top three movies of all-time.

Interstellar

Interstellar is the best movie I've ever seen. It makes us question the very realities of life, and features outstanding performances, particularly from Matthew McConaughey.

Good Will Hunting

The dialogue, performances, and character arcs in Good Will Hunting are exceptionally well done. The characters feel incredibly authentic

Gone Girl

The movie is masterfully portrayed. Ben Affleck's unexpected framing by Rosamund Pike adds an intriguing twist, keeping viewers captivated and curious about the conclusion throughout.
